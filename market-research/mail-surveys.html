The Power of Print in a Digital World

Mail surveys remain a highly effective research tool for specific populations and contexts — particularly older demographics, rural communities, healthcare patients, and situations where regulatory or compliance requirements demand a paper trail. NRG's mail survey capability covers end-to-end design, print, mail, data capture, and analysis.

What This Includes

  • Questionnaire design and print production
  • Mailing list procurement and management
  • Bulk mailing and postage
  • Freepost return envelopes
  • Data entry and verification
  • Response rate monitoring and follow-up
