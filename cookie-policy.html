Cookie Policy

Last updated: June 2026

This Cookie Policy explains how Nova Research Group uses cookies and similar tracking technologies on our website novaresearchgroup.site.

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are placed on your device when you visit a website. They are widely used to make websites work more efficiently and to provide information to website owners about how users interact with their site.

2. Types of Cookies We Use

Essential Cookies

These cookies are necessary for the website to function properly. They enable core functionality such as navigation and access to secure areas. The website cannot function properly without these cookies.

Analytics Cookies

We use analytics cookies to understand how visitors interact with our website. This helps us improve the site's performance and user experience. All data collected is aggregated and anonymised.

Functional Cookies

These cookies allow the website to remember choices you make (such as your preferred language) and provide enhanced, personalised features.

Marketing Cookies

Marketing cookies may be placed by our advertising partners to build a profile of your interests and show relevant adverts on other sites. They do not store personal information directly but identify your browser and device.

3. How to Control Cookies

You can control and/or delete cookies as you wish. You can delete all cookies that are already on your computer and you can set most browsers to prevent them from being placed. If you do this, however, you may have to manually adjust some preferences every time you visit a site.

To manage cookies in your browser:

  • Chrome: Settings → Privacy and security → Cookies and other site data
  • Firefox: Settings → Privacy & Security → Cookies and Site Data
  • Safari: Preferences → Privacy → Manage Website Data
  • Edge: Settings → Cookies and site permissions
