Focus Groups

Expert-moderated group discussions that reveal what surveys can't.

Service Overview

The Power of Qualitative Dialogue

Focus groups unlock the "why" behind the numbers — the emotions, motivations, and group dynamics that drive consumer behaviour. NRG's experienced moderators create a safe, engaging environment where participants feel comfortable sharing honest, unfiltered opinions.

We offer both in-person studio focus groups and online video focus groups, giving you flexibility in recruitment, geography, and budget.

What This Includes

  • Expert professional moderation (in-person & online)
  • Participant recruitment and screening
  • Discussion guide development
  • Video/audio recording and transcription
  • Thematic analysis and insight report
  • Client observation facilities or live streaming

Why It Matters

Quantitative surveys tell you what people do; focus groups tell you why. For new product development, brand positioning, advertising pre-testing, and communications research, qualitative insight is indispensable.
