Tracking Studies

Monitor brand health, attitudes, and behaviour change over time with confidence.

Service Overview

Measure Change. Diagnose Effectiveness.

Tracking studies give organisations the ability to monitor key metrics over time — understanding how brand health, customer attitudes, and market dynamics shift in response to marketing activity, competitive actions, and external events.

What This Includes

  • Brand health and equity tracking
  • Awareness, consideration, and usage (ACU) tracking
  • Advertising and campaign tracking
  • Customer satisfaction and NPS tracking
  • Wave-on-wave trend analysis
  • Competitive benchmarking

Why It Matters

A single research snapshot tells you where you are. A tracking programme tells you where you're going — and whether your investments in marketing, product, and experience are actually moving the needle.
