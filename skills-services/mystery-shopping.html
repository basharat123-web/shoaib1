Mystery Shopping

Objective, covert evaluation of your customer experience from the customer's perspective.

Service Overview

See Your Business Through Your Customers' Eyes

Mystery shopping is the most direct way to understand how your brand promise is delivered at the point of customer contact. ARG's national network of trained mystery shoppers conducts objective, covert evaluations of your locations, staff, and service standards.

What This Includes

  • Retail & hospitality mystery shopping
  • Call centre & digital channel evaluation
  • Compliance and standards auditing
  • Competitor benchmarking visits
  • Detailed scorecards and narrative reports
  • Photo and video evidence (where permitted)

Why It Matters

Internal audits and customer surveys show what customers report. Mystery shopping shows what actually happens — bridging the gap between policy and practice, and identifying the exact points where customer experience breaks down.
