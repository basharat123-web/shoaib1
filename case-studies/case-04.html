Brand Perception Tracking for a Regional Bank

Quarterly brand health monitoring against five competitors across three states.

Financial Services
Quarterly Online Tracking Study
600/wave (2,400/year)
Ongoing (18+ months)

Client Context

A regional bank with presence across three states wanted to systematically monitor its brand health and competitive positioning in response to a major new marketing campaign and the entrance of two new fintech competitors into its markets.

Research Approach

NRG designed a quarterly online tracking study with 600 adult bank customers per wave. The tracker measured brand awareness, consideration, preference, attribute associations, and Net Promoter Score for the client and five competitors. NRG delivers interactive dashboards within 5 business days of each fieldwork close.

Key Findings

The tracking data revealed that while the client's aided awareness remained strong, unaided awareness was declining among 25-40 year olds — the segment most actively targeted by fintech competitors. Attribute analysis showed the brand was losing ground on "digital banking convenience" while maintaining strength on "trust" and "local knowledge."

Business Impact

The client used NRG's tracking data to rebalance its marketing investment — shifting 35% of spend to digital channels targeting younger customers. Unaided awareness among 25-40s increased by 12 points over the following two quarters.
