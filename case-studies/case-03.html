New Product Concept Test for an FMCG Brand

Validating three product concepts before a major launch investment decision.

FMCG / Consumer Goods
Online Concept Test + Focus Groups
1,200 US consumers
5 weeks

Client Context

A leading FMCG company had developed three new product concepts in the health snack category and needed consumer validation before committing to a multi-million dollar launch investment. They needed to understand which concept had the strongest purchase intent, appeal, and differentiation.

Research Approach

NRG conducted a monadic concept test with 400 US consumers per concept, followed by two focus groups per concept to explore the qualitative "why" behind the quantitative scores. Each concept was evaluated on purchase intent, appeal, uniqueness, relevance, and value perceptions.

Key Findings

Concept 3 significantly outperformed on purchase intent (68% "definitely/probably would buy" vs 42% and 51% for concepts 1 and 2). Qualitative research revealed that the winning concept's success was driven by its distinctive flavour claim and packaging clarity — factors the client team had considered secondary.

Business Impact

The client launched Concept 3 with minor messaging refinements identified in the research. The product achieved distribution in 4,200 stores in the first six months and exceeded Year 1 revenue targets by 22%.
