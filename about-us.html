About Us

Axiom Research Group

Axiom Research Group (ARG) is an online platform that connects users with online surveys and market-research studies. We operate at the intersection of technology and research, building a high-quality panel of engaged participants while delivering enterprise-grade research solutions to our business clients.

Based in Buffalo, New York, USA, ARG has established itself as a reliable and transparent online sampling provider. Our platform focuses on genuine user participation, ethical data collection, and delivering the highest quality data to research buyers.

Who We Are

Reliable. Transparent. Ethical.

At ARG, we believe great research starts with genuine people sharing real opinions in a trustworthy environment.

Reliable Online Sampling

We maintain a carefully curated panel of verified participants, ensuring consistent quality and representativeness across all our research projects.

Genuine User Participation

Every respondent on our platform is a real person who has voluntarily opted in — no bots, no incentive fraud, no misleading practices.

Ethical Data Collection

We adhere to the highest ethical standards in data collection, fully compliant with GDPR, CCPA, and industry codes of conduct.

Our Sampling Approach

Building a High-Quality Panel

Quality data starts with quality participants. Our multi-stage approach ensures every respondent is genuine, engaged, and relevant.

All panel members opt in voluntarily. We never use deceptive recruitment tactics or misleading incentive structures.

No Bot Traffic
